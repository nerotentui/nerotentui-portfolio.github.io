Sunflower & Moon

Sunflower & Moon is an interactive web-based poetry. This poem aims to tell a story about a toxic relationship where one person is cheating on another. Viewers can see the feeling of deep despair, like a knife backstab, a betrayal. Agony and pain as each seconds pass by. A toxic relationship could form from neglect or abuse, but part of this is also from choosing the wrong people to be with.

Original poem by Sophie Kim
